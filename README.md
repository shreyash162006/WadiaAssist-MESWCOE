WadiaAssist is an Autonomous Academic Operations Agent designed for  
**MES Wadia College of Engineering (MESWCOE), Pune**.

It is not a chatbot.

It is a structured digital support system that helps students get
accurate answers, ensures their requests reach the right department,
and reduces administrative workload.

---

## Why WadiaAssist Exists

In colleges, students often face:
- Delayed responses
- Confusing procedures
- Repeated visits to offices
- No tracking of complaints or requests

WadiaAssist solves this by acting as a **single, reliable point of contact**
between students and the institution.

---

## What This System Does

- Answers student questions using verified college information
- Understands multiple questions asked together
- Asks clarifying questions when needed
- Creates official support tickets when issues require action
- Escalates sensitive or unclear matters responsibly
- Keeps track of deadlines and important academic events

---

## Designed For

- Students of MESWCOE
- Academic and administrative departments
- Institutional transparency and accountability

This system is built to work in the real world, not just as a demo.
